As an Atlanta based microcinema, Contraband Cinema is resource for filmmakers pursuing fine-art, essay, documentary, home movie, orphan, cult, animation and exploitation films and videos. We connect Atlanta audiences with the strange and wonderful sub-stream of underground cinema.

WE'LL BE BACK

Contrabandits! Forgive us. We are remaining quiet for a while, but we will return. We thank you for your patience while we recharge, regroup, and rekindle our love for showing you all weird and wonderful movies. In the meantime please continue to follow our friends Film Love, Wonderroot's Local Film Series, Buried Alive Film Fest, Atlanta Film Society, Eyedrum Art & Music Gallery, Yallywood Film Festival and all other lovely micro-cinema series happening in this fair town.
