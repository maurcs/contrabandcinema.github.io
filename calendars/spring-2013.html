Spring 2013

Sat. 3/2: IOWA Film MaFiA takes over

Saturday March 2nd 8pm at MINT gallery

The Iowa Film MaFiA takes over Contraband Cinema. Wielding the collective might of their curiosity, collaborations, teachings, and Iowa University MFAs the group brings experimental media for hacking into your eyeballs and altering your senses. Projection on 16mm and video will be accompanied by the filmmakers live and in person. Watch glorious 16mm 3D film with handmade 3D glasses.

See work by Evan Meany, scientist who hunts ghosts in the glitch; Jonathan Rattner, multimedia maker of memories and recorder of place; Deon Kay, professor extraordinaire and editor in chief, chiefly editing; Chris Renaud, designer of images and collector of time; Charlotte Taylor, inventor writing fairy tales in 3D optics.

Mon. 3/18: Atl FF Experimental Shorts

Saturday March 18th 9:30pm at Seven Stages

Come on down to the Atlanta Film Festival for this very special night of experimental film, hosted by Contraband Cinema.

Local, regional and international filmmakers will be present to screen and discuss their work in this 1 NIGHT ONLY event.

Films by Georg Koszulinski, Matteo Giovanelli, Christopher Harris, Evan Meaney, Walter Ungerer, Tianran Duan, Joseph Ernst, Rachel Moore, Robbie Land, Eric Patrick, Daniel Dietzel, Jillian Mayer & Lucas Leyva, and Kathryn Brillhart.
