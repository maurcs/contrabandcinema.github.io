CALL FOR ARTISTS - The Visitor Art Show

THE VISITOR Art Show
Friday, July 11th 7pm
Eyedrum Gallery, Atlanta, Georgia

Contraband Cinema invites artists to create work inspired by The Visitor, a supernatural sci-fi thriller based in and filmed on location in Atlanta, Georgia in 1979. Art will be on display Friday, July 11th at Eyedrum. On Saturday July, 12th there will be a special screening of The Visitor in the same space with special guests in attendance.

We are inviting all Atlanta artists to participate in this one-of-a-kind art exhibition that will feature new and original pieces based on or inspired by the film. The Visitor is overflowing with memorable scenes and surreal images that completely lend itself to be captured in any medium. The possibilities are endless!

For those who have not seen the film, The Visitor is available to rent on DVD and Blu-Ray at Atlanta’s Videodrome video store, or you can stream it through iTunes or Amazon Instant. Trust us. It’s well worth the rental fee. For information and ideas please see our inspiration art board and The Visitor website or contact us.

Additional details:

  • The event is being organized by Contraband Cinema.
  • This is a two night event. The art show will take place Friday, July the 11th at 7 PM. The screening will occur Saturday, July the 12th at 7 PM.
  • The deadline for your piece will be the week of July 7th. We would like you to bring your finished piece to Eyedrum or arrange a time with Eric to meet. More information on that as the date approaches.
  • There are no size requirements for the pieces, just make sure they are transportable. All media types are welcome.
  • Most importantly, we will not be selling any of the artwork at the show. However, if someone is interested in purchasing your piece, you can work with that person directly and discuss those details.
